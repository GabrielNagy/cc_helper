A hassle-free film reservation interface

So, this site takes event data from CinemaCity's API (sorry guys), puts it through a blender that extracts the good bits (CouchDB), then basically prints them out (Flask).

Features

  • you can open the reservation pages in a NEW TAB (WOW)
  • if you don't find nice seats, you can CLOSE the TAB and OPEN a NEW TAB at a different hour
  • you don't have to select the cinema every time you open the website
  • avoid loading unimportant data

For future development:

  • the ability to select the cinema (currently the app is tailored for Shopping City Timișoara
  • interesting statistics (once enough relevant data is collected, for now we have to make do with Timothée Chalamet)
  • automatic one-click reservations with Selenium or whatever technology one uses to make this stuff
  • make it prettier? don't know about that
